The Best Chocolate Chip Cookies

Total Time: 1hr 5 min

Prep Time: 20 minutes

Cook Time: 15 minutes

Makes 12 cookies

Ingredients

115g butter

100g granulated sugar

165g brown sugar

1 tsp salt

1 large egg

1 tsp vanilla extract

155g plain flour

1/2 tsp baking soda

110g milk chocolate

110g white chocolate

  1. Brown the butter in a large saucepan and let cool.
  2. Whisk browned butter with the sugars and salt until a paste forms with no lumps
  3. Whisk in the egg and vanilla, beating until light ribbons fall off the whisk and remain for a short while before falling back into the mixture.
  4. Sift in the flour and baking soda, then fold the mixture using a spatula. Do not overmix, as this will result in cakier cookies.
  5. Chop the chocolate into rough chunks and fold into the cookie dough.
  6. Chill the dough for at least 30 minutes.
  7. Preheat oven to 180 degrees celcius. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  8. Use an ice-cream scoop to scoop the dough onto the parchment paper-lined baking sheet, leaving at least 10cm of space between each cookie, and 5cm of space between the cookie and the edge of the pan.
  9. Bake for 12-15 minutes, until the edges have started to turn brown.
  10. Leave to cool completely before serving.
